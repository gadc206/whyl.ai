how it works

Every spinner is a
tiny opportunity.

AI wait times are unpredictable, unavoidable, and right now completely wasted. Whyl turns each one into tokens and a moment of genuine recovery.

01

Install & toggle on

Whyl lives as a Chrome extension beside your AI tools. Flip it on and it quietly watches for the "thinking" state in Claude, Cursor, Copilot, or ChatGPT.

02

The moment it stalls, a pitch appears

A single 15-second startup pitch slides into the chat box — right where you're already looking. No new tab, no popup, no context leaving the screen.

03

Tokens land in your balance

Finish the pitch and compute tokens drop straight into your wallet — redeemable for more prompts and longer agent runs across the tools you already use.

04

Longer waits get a micro-task

If the wait stretches, Whyl suggests a research-backed reset sized to the estimate — always something you can abandon the second output arrives.
